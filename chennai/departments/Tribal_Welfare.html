Hostel Repairs and Maintenance

As per G.O ms. No. 15 dated 15.03.2017, Rs. 13.59 lakhs were allotted for 10 hostels towards repairs and maintenance. Rs. 37.60 lakhs were sanctioned for repairs of 8 hostels.

Hostel Admission

According to G.O.79 dated 13.08.2012, 85% SC, 10% BC, and 5% OC students are admitted. Admission is based on selection by hostel committee members. Annual family income must be within 2 lakhs.

Required Documents for Hostel Admission

  • Community Certificate
  • Aadhar card
  • Ration Card
  • Bonafide Certificate
  • Bank Account details

Scholarships and Concessions

SC/ST girls studying from III to V std are given Rs. 500 per year, VI std Rs. 1000, and 7th & 8th std Rs. 1500 as incentives.

In 2017-18, Rs. 1599.106 lakhs were sanctioned as scholarships to 39,111 students in Chennai district. Rs. 1613.97 lakhs were allotted for this scheme for the year 2017-18.

Post metric and pre metric scholarships are sanctioned online. Particulars are submitted by Headmasters or Principals to the District ADW Office and forwarded to the Directorate, making ECS payments directly to student accounts.
